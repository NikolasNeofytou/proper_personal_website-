About Me

I am an ECE student at NTUA, with a specialization in electronics and control systems. In regard to my specialization courses, something more interesting has never come up beforeâ€”something more interesting will not come up again. The more time I devote to electronics and systems, the more I am assured this is the field I want to acquire mastery over, and naturally, it is the one through which I am able to materialize my will to power. With the help of my golden sponsors (my parents), I have already acquired a significant subset of lab material to work with in my spare time, with no horizon in sight.

In parallel with my studies, I have a great interest in the fields of history and philosophy. A special emphasis is given to the study of Roman politics throughout their historyâ€”from the early beginnings to the late Republic, to the eventual decline and fall. The classical age of Greece still beams the light of civilization, which can be encapsulated through their tragedies and philosophical works of Plato and Aristotle. My favourite philosophers of all time, Schopenhauer and Nietzsche, for me push the boundaries of elegance and original thought, with the latter even paying the price of insanity.

In pursuit of equilibrium between mind and body, I keep myself active through sport activities. I have played football from a young age and until recently played for a local team in Zografou. Now my main sport is tennis, which took me a while to learn the basics, but stillness and patience have kept me on the path of progress.

During the summer of 2025, I acquired a more quality camera and have loved every second of it. Now I get to capture my best personal moments, nature in more vivid detail, and even my electronics from a smaller distance using a macro lens. Not only is it the recipient of warming moments but also the agent of exploring new territories and places.
